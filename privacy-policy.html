Privacy Policy

Future Self Messages Chrome Extension

The Short Version

We collect nothing. All your messages stay on your device. No accounts, no servers, no tracking. Your data never leaves your browser.

Data Collection

Future Self Messages does not collect, transmit, store, or share any personal data or user information whatsoever.

We have no servers. We have no database. We have no analytics. We don't know who you are or what messages you write.

Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device using Chrome's built-in storage API (chrome.storage.local). This includes:

  • Messages you write to your future self
  • Scheduled delivery times
  • Your theme preference and settings
  • Draft messages (auto-saved while typing)

This data exists only on your computer and is never transmitted anywhere. If you uninstall the extension or clear your browser data, this information is permanently deleted.

Third-Party Services

This extension makes zero network requests. It does not use:

  • Analytics or tracking services
  • Advertising networks
  • Cloud storage or sync services
  • Any external APIs or servers

The extension works entirely offline after installation.

Your Rights

Since we don't collect any data, there's nothing to request, modify, or delete from our end. You have complete control over your data:

  • Export: Use the built-in export feature to save your messages as JSON
  • Delete: Use "Clear All Data" in settings, or simply uninstall the extension
  • Access: All your data is visible to you within the extension at all times

Changes to This Policy

If we ever update this privacy policy, we'll post the changes here and update the date below. But honestly, our commitment to collecting zero data isn't going to change.
